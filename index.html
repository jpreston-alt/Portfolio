Joanna Preston

Web Developer | Seattle, WA

Hi! My name’s Joanna, welcome to my site. I’m a designer, builder, and a soon to be full-stack web developer. I spent the last five years of my career in retail design working for Urban Outfitters as a Market Display Artist. My career in design has taught me how to learn quickly, creatively solve problems, and curate spaces for impactful user experience. I've developed a newfound passion for web development, and am currently working hard to transition into web dev as a career.

Currently I'm enrolled at the University of Washington’s Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp, and loving it. The constant problem solving is right up my alley, and learning to make things in a whole new medium is beyond exciting to me. So far, I’ve become proficient in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Bulma, JavaScript, jQuery, and AJAX, with more to come! I am excited about merging my background in design with my newly developed technical skills to build useful and beautiful things.

Outside of work and class, I enjoy riding my bike, reading, woodworking, and cooking. Other obsessions include Stuff You Should Know, coffee, Queen, my dog Bug, and now coding. Please check out my portfolio page to see what I’ve been working on lately!
